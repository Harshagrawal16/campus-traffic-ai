Mathematical Algorithm Formulation

The AI controller optimizes signal phases using a dynamic cost function based on vehicle queue delays:

\[ J = \sum_{i \in Lanes} \left( w_1 \cdot Q_i + w_2 \cdot W_i \right) \]

Where:

  • \(Q_i\): Queue length in lane \(i\) (measured by cameras).
  • \(W_i\): Maximum wait time of vehicle at front of lane \(i\).
  • \(w_1, w_2\): Weights defined in AI parameter profiles.

At each cycle selection, the algorithm activates the phase \(P^*\) which maximizes the rate of cost reduction:

\[ P^* = \arg\max_{P} \Delta J(P) \]
